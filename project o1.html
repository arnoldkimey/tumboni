EASTC
CONVOCATION CEREMONY

Welcome to 7Th Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center convocation ceremony where degrees are conferred upon a graduating class,which will be opened on 01 november 2024

Our theme

The 7th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians,students,alumni,benefactors,and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community.It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research and professional development.

The convocation has selected the topic The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November Africa Statistics Day.The theme stated as
Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA): The role of implementation of statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of africa.

Awarding prizes to the best students

An award to all graduands who attained a Distinction(for postgraduated) or first class (for undergraduate).The award was being a certificate of Appreciation from the EASTC Senate.But another award prize presented as,
The rector's prizes:An award to the student with the highest GPA overall amongst the graduands of both levels.
Academic staff's prize:Awarded to a student with outstanding performance on subjects and for female student with highest GPA overall amongst the graduands and outstanding performance in research project
Certificates of merit to EASTC-SO leaders:Awarded to the leadership of student organization for their tireless efforts and will to add administrative tasks to their heavy academic loads.The award will be a certificate for each leader.

Presentation

Reflected on the challenges and triumphs of the graduating class as a whole,Discuss the importance of resilience and perseverance in achieving success. Give practical advice for life after high school ,such as financial management or maintaining relationships.
